Inter-relation between Veteran Affairs expenditure, Ethnicity and Joint Commission/CARF Commission Accredited Facilities per state.

Description:

In this project we compare the medical expense of veterans based on etnicity. The project also determines how state wise expenditure of VA Affairs depends on ethnicity and commission accredited facilities.

1. We are calculating the medical expenditure of veterans in each state

2. It also compares the medical expense of veteran based on ethnicity.

3. It provides information of expenditure on commission accredited facilities.

Who is Interested in this information:

1. Veteran Health care officials who need information about the medical expenditure of the veterans.

2. Health insurance companies which needs statistics on medical expense of US veterans.

3. State Government and Accreditation agencies providing licenses and Medicaid reimbursements.
